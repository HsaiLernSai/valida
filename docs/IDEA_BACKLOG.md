# Valida Idea Backlog

Everything here is unimplemented unless explicitly moved to `ROADMAP.md` as completed. Ordering is thematic, not a commitment.

## Discovery and sharing

- QR customization and printable recruitment cards.
- Server-backed public links for newly created research after authentication and persistence are approved.
- Functional feed search, sorting, following, hashtag filters, and recommendations.
- Research collections, saved searches, topic subscriptions, and curated lists.
- Research templates by goal, industry, and method.
- Embeddable request cards and organization-branded campaign pages.

## Research and forms

- Conditional form logic, sections/pages, scales, ranking, date, upload, consent, and contact-permission fields.
- Draft autosave, duplicate research, reusable question library, import/export, and response editing policy.
- Verified Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, and Typeform integrations.
- Research verification for creator identity, methods, links, incentives, or ethics review.
- Participant screening, qualification rules, quotas, scheduling, and interview calendar integrations.
- Multilingual authoring and cached auto-translation for English, Thai, Myanmar, and Chinese.

## Community and communication

- Notifications, comments, threaded discussion, direct messaging, and creator announcements.
- Follow creators/topics, research collections, mentions, and collaborative research teams.
- Reporting, moderation queues, trust signals, reputation, anti-spam controls, and admin dashboard.
- Organization accounts, team roles, shared workspaces, and approval workflows.

## Participation and incentives

- Credit system, transparent ledger, earning/spending rules, and creator boosts.
- Reward system, incentives, gift cards/payout integrations, eligibility rules, and fraud prevention.
- Participation streaks or milestones only if they improve research quality rather than encourage low-effort answers.
- Consent receipts, privacy controls, data deletion/export, and participation availability preferences.

## Insights and analytics

- Creator analytics dashboard: views, opens, starts, completions, conversion, time-to-response, and drop-off.
- Response exports, filters, coding/tagging, cross-tabs, cohorts, and research repository.
- AI response summary, AI insights, theme extraction, contradiction detection, and suggested follow-up questions.
- AI-assisted research brief, question quality review, bias warnings, and audience suggestions.
- Benchmarks by research goal and target audience with privacy-safe aggregation.

## Platform and operations

- Real profiles, authentication, backend database, cross-device state, realtime updates, and audit logs.
- Webhooks, API access, integrations, billing, subscriptions, and organization administration.
- Mobile apps or a polished installable PWA after core web retention is proven.
- Data retention, backups, observability, abuse response, localization operations, and accessibility certification.
- Research marketplace, managed recruitment, verified participant panels, and enterprise governance.

## Ideas needing product decisions first

- Whether credits are monetary, promotional, or reputation-based.
- Whether anonymous participation is allowed and how one-person-one-response is enforced.
- Whether participants can edit or withdraw submitted responses.
- How creators close, reopen, archive, or delete research.
- Whether hashtags remain flat discovery labels or gain moderation/following semantics.
- What “verified within Valida” guarantees once real identity and backend persistence exist.
